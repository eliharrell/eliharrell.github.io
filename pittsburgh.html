ELI HARRELL

Pittsburgh residential housing

a computer ~ 3D and hand ~ 2D project

The culmination of the class "Pittsburgh's Common Buildings." With Shivani Chatterji and Jasmina Scekic, chose a site in Pittsburgh's South Side Slopes neighborhood. Currently, our site is empty save for a billboard, but due to rising demand for housing in the area, we proposed this multi-family unit on that land.
