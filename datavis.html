D3 Data Visualization

a computer ~ 2D project

I was tasked with creating a better visualization of the inscrutable Asics advertisement below. My design locates the injury rates on a "body map," since it would be the most legible—everyone can find their knee, but not everyone can read a radial diagram. Like precipitation radar shown on TV, I used a banded colormap to allow the viewer to quickly discriminate the overall intensity of a value in a given location, as well as using the legend to look up the exact value.

Alternatively, in acting against every dictum of good data visualization, I produced an even more cryptic representation of the Asics data. Both versions are linked below.
