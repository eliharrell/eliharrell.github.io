WHPK 88.5FM portable radio booth

This booth was commissioned by the campus radio station of the University of Chicago, which serves the adjacent neighborhoods of (W)oodlawn, (H)yde (P)ark, and (K)enwood.

The booth is meant to bring the cozy plywood interior of the station out to the world for live events.
