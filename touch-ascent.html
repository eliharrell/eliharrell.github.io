ELI HARRELL

Touch Ascent

a hand ~ 2D project

What would a room that stimulated your sense of balance look like? Perhaps a long ramp of different textures and slopes, gaining in traction and in steepness as one ascends; a "touch ascent." This was a chance to do some real architectural speculation, and to work on drawing at different scales simultaneously.
