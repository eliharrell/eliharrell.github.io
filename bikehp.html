Bike Hyde Park poster

a computer ~ 2D project

On a smooth road, a bicycle is the most energy efficient mode of travel . Potholes complicate this. Despite a city-wide interest in increasing the accessibility of cycling, there weren't any maps of road texture. So I created my own, taking a block-by-block survey of Hyde Park on my bike, noting down the average texture I observed between each corner. Simple symbology illustrates the varying road textures in even a small neighborhood.
