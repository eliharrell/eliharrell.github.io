ELI HARRELL

Doc Films

computer ~ 2D and hand ~ 2D projects

Doc Films is the nation's oldest student-run film society, and really runs an entire movie theater top to bottom. Check out their website to see what they're up to currently. As the Design Chair (spring 2022 – winter 2024), I was responsible for the organization's aesthetic as well as print advertising. Each quarter of the academic year (including summers), I created a print calendar and accompanying booklet with all the screening information for that term.

Autumn 2023 program

~

To make up for a small budget, the calendar and the booklet became one. To fit the approximately 11,000 words total onto a single sheet of 22.75" by 26.25" paper, I looked to tabloid designs, which truly excel at this. Text becomes its own kind of image, a substance to mold on each page.

Doc Films DOC90 logo

~

To commemorate the 90th anniversary of the founding of Doc Films ("Documentary Film Group," in 1932), we needed a new logo, marking a new era of the organization. Although as I write this (in 2023), the logo is now outdated, it has remained a key part of the organization's image because of its recognizability and iconographic representation of a film reel. It is Doc Films, after all. 		the final logo
